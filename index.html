The food lover history

More than 25+ years of experiance

At Food Lover, our journey didn't start with a business plan; it started with a passion for bringing people together through authentic taste. For over 25 years, we have been a proud part of Cairo’s vibrant culinary landscape.

What began as a small vision a quarter-century ago has grown into a cornerstone of the community. Through the decades, our commitment has remained simple and unchanging: to source the finest ingredients and prepare them with the care and tradition that Cairo deserves. Whether you are a local regular or a visitor exploring the city, Food Lover is where you go for a meal that feels like home. Thank you for letting us serve you for 25 wonderful years. We can’t wait to welcome you to our table for many more.
